💡 How do I download Yesterday's Sales Report?
Go to the Sales Reports tab from the sidebar menu, and click on the Download CSV link located at the top-right of the Recent Transactions section.
🛠️ How is the Sales Share Pie Chart calculated?
The pie chart dynamically aggregates the sales counts of all items sold today and displays their percentage share relative to the total quantity sold today.
🚪 How do I switch views to verify Student ordering flows?
Click the Switch to Student View button located at the bottom of the sidebar to instantly access the student food ordering layout.
